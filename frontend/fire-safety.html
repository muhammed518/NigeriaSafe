Fire Safety

Know what to do before, during, and after a fire.

Before a Fire

Create and practice a fire escape plan. Install smoke alarms on every level of your home, inside bedrooms and outside sleeping areas. Test them every month. Keep flammable items like paper, clothing, and curtains away from heat sources.

During a Fire

If a fire occurs, get out and stay out. Crawl low under smoke to your exit. Feel doors for heat before opening them. If the fire is small and you are trained, use a fire extinguisher. Otherwise, evacuate immediately and call for help from outside.

After a Fire

Do not re-enter a fire-damaged building until it has been declared safe by authorities. Contact your local disaster relief service for help with immediate needs. Check with the fire department to make sure your utilities are safe to use.

Landslide Safety

How to prepare for and react to a landslide.

Before a Landslide

Know if you live in an area prone to landslides. Look for warning signs such as new cracks in pavement or foundations, leaning trees or poles, and changes in water flow. Have an evacuation plan and an emergency kit ready.

During a Landslide

Evacuate immediately if you suspect a landslide is imminent or if authorities issue an order. Move to higher ground away from the slide's path. If you cannot escape, curl into a tight ball and protect your head and neck with your arms.

After a Landslide

Stay away from the slide area as additional slides can occur. Check for injured or trapped persons near the slide without entering the slide area directly. Report broken utility lines and damaged roadways to the authorities. Listen to local radio for the latest emergency information.
